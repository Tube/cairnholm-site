The Séance

Each week Lady Lavinia Loveless holds a sitting at Cruikshank Manor, for the comfort of the Colonel and the improvement of her purse. The lamps are put out; the circle is joined; the dead oblige. Every account of the dark is sworn true — and one is an invention.
