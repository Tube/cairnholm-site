The Vespers

Before the evening office, St Serf’s rings a touch. The band is chronically short, and guests of the parish take the vacant ropes; the method is unforgiving, and every hand upon a rope is a hand accounted for — every two seconds, for the whole of the touch. The tower proves everyone. The murder lives in the flaw.
