Being the daily record of

The Cairnholm
Murder Club

No. — Cairnholm, MDCCCLXXXVI Price One Penny
❦

The first case is at press.

The Club begs the reader’s patience: the dead of Cairnholm are punctual to a fault, but the printers keep their own hours. A fresh murder will be laid before you each morning — and the truth of it, in Inspector Ulysses’s hand, the morning after.

Return presently; somebody here is not long for the page.
